Redefining the future of soccer

19 Football Academy is an academy setup to provide technical, tactical, physical and mental skill for young players who are capable of competing in various levels.

our mission

To create a supportive environment where young footballers can develop their skill with a culture of excellence and discipline.

our vision

To become a lead football academy with vibrant and confident young players.

An assembly of champions

We don't sell dreams: we build them. One drop of sweat at a time. One cascade of teardrop after the other. We blend blood, sweat and tears into countless victories. We are the ultimate alchemists. We are 19 Football Academy.

What we offer:

Quality football without the politics. Affordable sessions, volunteer coaches, and a no-cut policy. We train skills while building character; every player gets a fair shot. Join the pitch where everyone knows your name

  • Twice-Daily Sessions

    Morning focuses on technical drills: first touch, passing accuracy, and ball control. Evenings are for tactical play: small-sided games and position-specific scenarios. All sessions are filmed for review. Players receive weekly progress reports from their assigned coach.

  • Position-Specific Training

    Strikers work on finishing and movement. Defenders train 1v1 duels and aerial dominance. Midfielders develop passing range and transition play. Goalkeepers get specialized shot-stopping and distribution drills. Coaches are assigned by position.

  • Injury Prevention

    Daily dynamic warm-ups and cool-downs are mandatory. Our physio team designs individualized mobility routines. Recovery sessions include stretching, foam rolling, and hydration checks.

  • Futsal Integration

    Weekly futsal sessions improve close control, quick thinking, and spatial awareness. The smaller, heavier ball develops superior technique. Sessions are mandatory and supervised by futsal-certified coaches.

A program for everyone

We offer a range of training programs to suit every player, from beginners to advanced levels. Whether you want to improve your skills, learn the game, or take your performance to the next level, we have a program for you.

  • junior academy

    The Junior Academy is an excellent starting point for children with the passion for soccer. They are taught the basics and necessary skills to set them in the right direction and build a solid foundation.

  • u15 & u17 academy

    Our U15 & U17 Academy mines, refines and builds upon the talents and skills of young players. Great sportsmanship skills are taught and passion is stirred into a blazing furnace of energy and excellent delivery.

  • goalkeeper training

    The goalkeeper training program teaches the skills, tactics and knowledge required for excelling in goalkeeping. The program begins with the basics and delves into more advanced strategies and techniques.

  • Elite Development Track

    Our Elite Development Track is designed for players who aspire to compete at the highest levels.Players receive mentorship, performance analysis, and support to help them reach their full potential and pursue opportunities in professional football.
